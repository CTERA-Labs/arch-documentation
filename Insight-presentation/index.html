Architecture and Business Model Evolution

The Strategic Imperative for Change

Architectural Limitations

Certain advanced capabilities are becoming difficult to implement, including:

  • Real-time analytics
  • AI Functionalities
  • SIEM capabilities

Market Gaps

We lack a compelling solution for highly regulated customers with strict data residency and sovereignty requirements, limiting our market reach.

Core Capabilities for the Next-Generation Platform

  • Real time analytics dashboard: Low-latency, interactive monitoring.
  • AI Functionalities: Anomaly detection, RCA, and conversational search (RAG).
  • Security Analytics (SIEM): Real-time threat detection from audit logs.
  • Long-Term Analytics: Deep analysis of historical data for compliance and trends.
  • Architectural Flexibility: A cloud-agnostic, open-source foundation.

Proposed Architecture: OpenSearch

A community-driven, open-source search and analytics suite, ideal for log analytics, real-time monitoring, and security.
